Fruit Information

Apple

Apples are among the most popular fruits, known for their crisp texture and sweet-tart flavor. They are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants. Eating apples can be beneficial for heart health and aid in weight loss.

Banana

Bananas are a great source of potassium, vitamin C, and vitamin B6. They are perfect for a quick energy boost, making them an ideal snack before or after workouts. Bananas also promote digestive health due to their high fiber content.

Orange

Oranges are famous for their high vitamin C content, which boosts the immune system. They are juicy, tangy, and refreshing. Oranges also contain various antioxidants and are low in calories, making them an excellent choice for a healthy diet.

Strawberry

Strawberries are delicious and nutrient-rich, packed with vitamins, fiber, and antioxidants. They have a low glycemic index, making them suitable for people with diabetes. Strawberries are also known to support heart health and improve blood sugar control.
