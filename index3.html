About Us

Hungy? Want to eat delicious momos! Visit King of Momos!

King Of Momo is a popular chain of quick-service restaurants that specializes in serving momos, a popular street food from the Indian subcontinent. Founded in Kolkata, India in 2008 by Sagar Daryani and Binod Homagai, King Of Momo has since expanded to become one of the largest fast-food chains in India, with over 500 outlets across the country. King Of Momo has become known for its affordable prices and wide variety of momo options, making it a popular destination for both college students and families alike. Overall, King Of Momo is a popular and beloved fast-food chain in India and beyond, known for its delicious momos and commitment to quality and sustainability. The address of this restaurant is Shop No G39, Ground Floor, Cosmos Square, Chikhali Dongri Road, Global City, Virar, Virar, Maharashtra.

Momos served with delicious spicy red chutney and white mayo.
